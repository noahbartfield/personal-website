About Me.

Full-Stack Software Developer

For most of my life I have focused my energy and creativity on a career in music. In the years since college, my life has been filled with exciting moments as well as moments of questioning whether the up and down nature of a career in music was best for me. I had heard positive rumblings about Nashville Software School and decided to make the leap. What immediately drew me to software development was the focus on continual learning and growth. This spoke to my curiosity. What then captured me further was similar to what I have always loved about music - there is nothing more fulfilling than making something out of nothing. I am proud to consider myself a software developer and am thankful for the path that brought me here.
